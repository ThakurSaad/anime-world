Brief History Of Anime

INVENTION

Before the advent of film, Japan already had a rich tradition of entertainment with colourful painted figures moving across the projection screen.

FATHER OF ANIME

Osamu Tezuka was a Japanese manga artist, cartoonist, and animator. His pioneering techniques, and innovative redefinitions of genres earned him such titles as "the Father of Anime".

EVOLUTION

The evolution of anime occurred in stages, starting with cutout animation and silent short films.

Future Of Anime

The global anime market size was reached at US$ 22.6 billion in 2020. It is predicted to be worth around US$ 48.3 billion by 2030

Aniamation Industry In Bangladesh

Bangladeshi animation is a new form of visual arts experiencing a rapid growth. The animation industry employs about 12,000 people in Bangladesh. Most of the animations produced are for outsourced projects from western nations.
