Brief History Of Anime

INVENTION

Before the advent of film, Japan already had a rich tradition of entertainment with colourful painted figures moving across the projection screen

FATHER OF ANIME

Osamu Tezuka was a Japanese manga artist, cartoonist, and animator. His pioneering techniques, and innovative redefinitions of genres earned him such titles as "the Father of Manga"

EVOLUTION

The evolution of anime occurred in stages, starting with cutout animation and silent short films.

Future Of Anime

The global anime market size was reached at US$ 22.6 billion in 2020 is predicted to be worth around US$ 48.3 billion by 2030

Discover The Global Community

Despite being of Japanease origin, anime has reached hundreds of millions of people across the globe. Our community is still on its teenage & going through an exponential growth.
